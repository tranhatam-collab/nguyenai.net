## Alternative: Direct IP Transfer

Instead of the HoldCo license model above, the parties may choose a **direct IP transfer**:

1. HoldCo transfers all Gen1/Gen2 IP to Nguyen AI operating entity
2. Nguyen AI pays a one-time purchase price of [X] USD
3. Nguyen AI owns Gen1/Gen2 outright
4. HoldCo retains no ongoing rights or revenue share

**Trade-offs:**
- Simpler structure (no ongoing license fees)
- Higher upfront cost
- Nguyen AI bears all IP risk
- Better for investors (clean IP ownership)
- Worse if Nguyen AI fails (no IP recovery for HoldCo)

**Recommendation:** Direct IP transfer is preferred for the 750K USD raise. Investors will want clean IP ownership in the entity they invest in. The HoldCo license model is a fallback if the founder wants to retain Gen1/Gen2 for other verticals.
