# Nguyễn AI / Nguyen AI — Product Architecture

> Subordinate to `docs/NGUYEN_AI_COMPUTER_MASTER_POSITIONING_GEN1_GEN2.md`.

## Product category

Nguyen AI Computer is a specialized cloud AI Computer line for the global Nguyen ecosystem. It is NOT only a genealogy site, NOT only a heritage network, NOT a chatbot, and NOT a generic AI tool catalog.

Each user owns a private AI Computer Instance with multi-model intelligence, an Agent team, tools, memory, data vault, workflows, evidence, approval gates and a secure execution environment.

## Nguyen Super Apps

### 1. Nguyen Roots — Cội Nguồn

Private genealogy workspace.

Must support:

- private family trees;
- multiple family branches;
- aliases, húy, tự and common names;
- historical and modern place names;
- family timelines;
- controlled GEDCOM import/export;
- living/deceased status;
- role-based access;
- invited relative confirmation;
- conflicting data records.

### 2. Nguyen Memory — Di Sản

Digital heritage archive.

Must support:

- photos, documents, audio, video;
- oral history interviews;
- provenance per artifact;
- access control per item;
- branch-scoped archives;
- private family archive by default;
- publish-to-network with consent;
- migration from existing family archives.

### 3. Nguyen Knowledge — Tri Thức

Bilingual edited knowledge base.

Must support:

- bilingual articles (VI/EN);
- source citation per claim;
- editor review workflow;
- dispute and discussion;
- public read, editor write;
- structured knowledge graph;
- cross-link to Roots and Trust.

### 4. Nguyen Trust — Minh Chứng

Claim, source, evidence, consent, dispute system.

Must support:

- claim record;
- source record;
- evidence record;
- verification status;
- confidence label;
- dispute record;
- edit history;
- audit log;
- publication rights.

### 5. Nguyen Network — Kết Nối

Verified community and diaspora network.

Must support:

- individual profiles;
- expert profiles;
- founder profiles;
- chapter profiles;
- diaspora connections;
- events;
- trusted connections;
- opt-in discovery.

### 6. Nguyen Founders — Sáng Lập

Founder, expert, business and project network.

Must support:

- founder profiles;
- business profiles;
- project profiles;
- mentorship matching;
- partnership board;
- opportunity board;
- investor readiness pack integration.

### 7. Nguyen Chapter OS — Chi Họ và Cộng Đồng

Operating system for branches, associations and communities.

Must support:

- member management;
- governance;
- events;
- documents;
- funds and minutes;
- private chapter AI;
- dedicated chapter website;
- permission and audit.

## Privacy defaults

- Living-person data is private by default.
- Family trees are private by default.
- Family documents are private until owner publishes.
- Founder profiles are public only after owner approval.
- AI Computer Instance data is private to the owner.
- Private app routes must be authenticated, server-side access controlled, noindex and excluded from public sitemaps.
- Approval gates required before sensitive actions.

## Evidence and trust

Every important piece of information must have:

- source;
- evidence;
- provider;
- verification status;
- edit history;
- dispute;
- publication rights;
- audit log.

Required labels: verified, primary source, secondary source, according to branch genealogy, oral history, insufficient evidence, disputed, cannot conclude.
