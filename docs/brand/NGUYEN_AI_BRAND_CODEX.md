# Nguyễn AI / Nguyen AI — Brand Codex

## Naming rules

Approved:

- Nguyễn AI in Vietnamese copy.
- Nguyen AI in English copy.
- nguyenai in code identifiers, package names and URLs.
- nguyenai.net as the canonical domain.

Not approved as brand names:

- Nguyên AI
- AI Nguyen
- NguyenAI
- Nguyễn.AI
- Nguyen Artificial Intelligence
- NAI Network

The word "Nguyên" may be used only in philosophy or editorial copy, not as the brand name.

## Color tokens

| Token | Hex | Role |
|---|---:|---|
| heritage-red | #7A1F2B | Heritage, mark, focus |
| deep-indigo | #0F2742 | Knowledge, technology, trust |
| bronze-gold | #C89B3C | Archive, bronze, highlight |
| jade-green | #1F6D5A | Life, generations, growth |
| parchment | #F4EBDD | Archival paper background |
| ink | #161A1D | Primary text |
| warm-white | #FFFDF8 | Light surface |

All components must be tested against WCAG 2.2 contrast targets.

## Typography

- Primary sans-serif: Be Vietnam Pro or equivalent Vietnamese-safe sans-serif.
- Editorial serif: Noto Serif or equivalent Vietnamese-safe serif.
- Do not use calligraphic fonts for long-form content.
- Use historical fonts only as visual accents.
- Keep font weights limited for performance.

## UI principles

- Public research content must be readable without JavaScript.
- Private family data must never appear in public static output.
- Evidence and source states must be visually distinct.
- Confidence labels must not imply certainty beyond evidence.
- CTAs must not promise discovery of ancestry or royal lineage.

## Canonical brand assets
