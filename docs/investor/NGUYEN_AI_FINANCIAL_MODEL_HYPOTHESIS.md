# Nguyễn AI — 5-Year Financial Model Hypothesis

**Status:** Hypothesis only. Not a forecast. Subject to change based on real traction, AI costs and market validation.

## Assumptions

| Assumption | Value |
|---|---|
| Identity audience | 33-34M |
| Year 1 target users | 1,000-5,000 |
| Year 3 target users | 50,000-150,000 |
| Year 5 target users | 300,000-800,000 |
| Free-to-paid conversion | 3-7% |
| Average revenue per paid user (ARPU) | 150,000-400,000 VND/month |
| Chapter ARPU | 2,990,000 VND/month |
| Gross margin target | 65-75% |
| AI cost per active account | Declining over time |

## Revenue hypothesis (VND, annual)

| Year | Subscription | Add-on services | Total revenue | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Year 1 | 200-800M | 50-150M | 250-950M | MVP, founding families |
| Year 2 | 1.5-5B | 300-900M | 1.8-5.9B | Chapter pilots, diaspora |
| Year 3 | 6-20B | 1.5-5B | 7.5-25B | Paid growth, chapter SaaS |
| Year 4 | 18-55B | 5-15B | 23-70B | Scale, white-label |
| Year 5 | 40-120B | 12-35B | 52-155B | Enterprise, API, global |

## Unit economics targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| CAC (consumer) | <300,000 VND |
| CAC (chapter) | <5,000,000 VND |
| LTV:CAC (consumer) | >4:1 |
| LTV:CAC (chapter) | >8:1 |
| Monthly churn (consumer) | <8% |
| Monthly churn (chapter) | <3% |
| Gross margin | >65% |

## Break-even hypothesis

- Consumer break-even: Year 3-4.
- Chapter break-even: Year 2-3.
- Platform break-even: Year 4-5.

## Sensitivity

| Scenario | Year 3 revenue | Year 5 revenue |
|---|---:|---:|
| Conservative | 5-10B | 30-60B |
| Base | 10-18B | 60-100B |
| Optimistic | 18-25B | 100-155B |

## Important caveats

- These are hypotheses for investor discussion, not commitments.
- AI costs may shift significantly.
- Vietnam payment behavior differs from global SaaS.
- Chapter sales cycle may be longer than consumer.
- Privacy and legal compliance costs are non-negotiable.
- Do not present these numbers as verified projections.
