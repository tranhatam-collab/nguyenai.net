## 2. Why the market is ready

AI agents are getting strong enterprise attention, but most organizations have not yet scaled successfully.

- McKinsey: 62% of surveyed organizations are at least experimenting with AI agents, but nearly two-thirds have not scaled AI enterprise-wide, and only 39% report EBIT impact at enterprise level. Source: McKinsey State of AI 2025.
- Microsoft: 46% of leaders report their organization has used agents to automate workflows or processes. Source: Microsoft 2025 Work Trend Index.
- Gartner: predicts over 40% of agentic AI projects may be canceled before end of 2027 due to rising costs, unclear ROI or weak governance; but by 2028, 33% of enterprise software applications may contain agentic AI. Source: Gartner press release June 2025.

This confirms the opportunity for Nguyen AI is not in claiming "we have Agents," but in the ability to:

- run real workflows;
- control costs;
- store memory;
- request approval;
- provide evidence;
- audit all actions;
- protect data;
- prove ROI per user group.

Vietnam population 2024: ~100.99 million (World Bank). A 2024 onomastics study estimates the Nguyen surname at ~31.57% in the modern dataset analyzed, implying a theoretical identity audience of **~32 million people** in Vietnam (102,3M × 31,57% ≈ 32,3M as of 2025). Source: MDPI.

The real value of "Nguyen" is:

> The Nguyen name is the door into the market; AI Computer utility is the reason customers stay and pay.

## 7. The decisive valuation issue: IP ownership

This is the most important point when fundraising.

Investors will not attribute the full value of `computer.iai.one` and `maytinhai.org` to Nguyen AI unless it is clear:

- who owns the engine;
- who owns the source code;
- how long Nguyen AI has usage rights;
- whether those rights are exclusive;
- whether rights transfer with an investment transaction;
- whether Nguyen AI continues to operate if the ecosystem structure changes.

### Recommended structure

#### Best option

A HoldCo owns Gen1 and Gen2, then grants Nguyen AI:

- long-term usage rights;
- exclusive vertical rights;
- commercialization rights;
- derivative module development rights;
- transfer rights with investment transactions;
- clear SLA;
- transparent internal fees.

Or transfer necessary IP rights directly into the Nguyen AI legal entity.

> If IP remains ambiguous, investors will value Nguyen AI as a dependent brand, not an independent technology company.

Vietnam Personal Data Protection Law No. 91/2025/QH15 took effect on 01/01/2026, establishing rights including the right to know, consent, access, correct and request data deletion. Source: Ministry of Public Security.

For Nguyen AI, privacy is not just compliance. It is:

- product capability;
- enterprise requirement;
- trust moat;
- brand protection;
- valuation protection.

Each AI Computer Instance must default to:
